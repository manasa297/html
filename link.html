WhatsApp

WhatsApp, founded in 2009 by Brian Acton and Jan Koum, revolutionized the way people communicate with each other. This messaging app, acquired by Facebook in 2014, has become an indispensable tool for billions of users worldwide.

With its user-friendly interface, end-to-end encryption, and features like group chats, voice and video calls, and file sharing, WhatsApp has transformed the way we connect, share, and interact with each other. Whether it's staying in touch with loved ones, collaborating with colleagues, or simply sharing moments from our daily lives, WhatsApp has become an integral part of our digital lives.
