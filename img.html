MOTHER

A mother is not just a person, she's a feeling, a warmth, a guiding light. She's the one who wipes away our tears, calms our fears, and soothes our souls. With a gentle touch, a loving word, and a selfless act, she shows us what unconditional love looks like. From the moment we're born, she's there, nurturing us, caring for us, and sacrificing for us. She's our first teacher, our first friend, and our first role model.

She teaches us to walk, to talk, to share, and to care. 
As we grow, she's the one who encourages us to chase our dreams, to take risks, and to never give up. She's the one who believes in us, even when we don't believe in ourselves. She's the one who reminds us of our strengths, our passions, and our purpose.
A mother's love is pure, selfless, and unwavering.
It's a love that's always there, no matter what. It's a love that heals, that inspires, and that transforms. It's a love that makes us feel seen, heard, and valued.

So let's take a moment to appreciate our mothers, to thank them, and to celebrate them. Let's honor their love, their devotion, and their sacrifices. Let's show them that we see them, we hear them, and we value them."
